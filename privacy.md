# Privacy Policy  
My French Bread  

Last updated: February 2026  

My French Bread (“the App”) respects your privacy.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account registration.

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Payment information

All saved bakeries, notes, ratings, and photos are stored locally on your device.

## 2. Location Access

The App may request access to your device location to help you pin bakeries accurately on the map.

Location data:
- Is used only within the App
- Is not transmitted to external servers
- Is not shared with third parties

You can disable location access anytime in your device settings.

## 3. Photos

If you choose to add photos, they are stored locally on your device.  
The App does not upload or store photos on external servers.

## 4. Analytics

The App may use anonymous usage statistics provided by app store platforms to understand general download and usage trends.

No personally identifiable information is collected.

## 5. Third-Party Links

The App may include links to external websites related to bakeries.  
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external websites.

## 6. Data Security

All personal memories, ratings, and photos remain on your device.  
We do not operate servers that store user content.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.  
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
